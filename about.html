ABOUT PATHAN

Pathaan is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Siddharth Anand and produced by Aditya Chopra. The film stars Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone and John Abraham, alongside Dimple Kapadia and Ashutosh Rana. It is the fourth instalment in the YRF Spy Universe, and is Khan's first film as a lead actor since Zero (2018). Written by Shridhar Raghavan, Abbas Tyrewala and Anand, the film is about Pathaan, an exiled RAW agent, who must take down Jim, a former RAW agent-turned-rogue leader of a private terror organisation, who is planning to spread a deadly lab-generated virus across India
